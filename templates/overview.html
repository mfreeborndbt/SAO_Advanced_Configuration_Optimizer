A quick guide to each tab and what to expect

7-day window: All data shown across tabs is from the past 7 days of scheduled job runs.
Scheduled jobs only: Merge jobs, CI jobs, and ad-hoc runs are excluded from the analysis.
Cost estimates are approximate: Dollar amounts are rough estimates meant to show order of magnitude — how much more expensive one job or model is compared to another — rather than exact spend.

Full Model Context

A comprehensive table of every table and incremental model in the project, combining run metrics, cost estimates, row-change trends, lineage, and SAO configuration into a single sortable and filterable view.

Data Dictionary

Definitions for every column and metric used across the dashboard, organized by tab so you can quickly look up what any field means.

Models Optimization

Surfaces table models that are strong candidates for conversion to incremental, based on size, cost, primary-key availability, and date-column detection.

Jobs Optimization

Analyzes scheduled jobs for overlap and redundancy, showing which jobs share models and where consolidation could reduce unnecessary builds.

Updated At

Identifies models with static or rarely-changing upstream dependencies, highlighting where SAO's updates_on setting can skip unnecessary rebuilds.

Build After

Shows how frequently each model rebuilds relative to its data-change rate, helping you set SAO's build_after threshold to prevent excessive runs.
